Moodboard Social App

McHacks, personal project



Role → Graphic design

Deliverables → Application design, visual identity



Moodboard was created as a "demo project", intended for participants at McGill's annual hackathon (McHacks) to learn, through an innteractive workshop, an introduction to UI/UX design and advanced Figma tools for mockups and project demos like prototyping. So here is Moodboard, a social app for designers and artists looking to share their work and connect with other artists. *I do not own the rights to the example works in the design*
