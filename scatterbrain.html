Scatterbrain Magazine is a Montréal and New York City based art, fashion, photography and music magazine aiming to bring attention to up and coming artists, musicians, designers and other creatives, as well as shed light to all that the cities have to offer. The pages of the magazine are inspired by new and exciting ideas, unconfined by a specific aesthetic or set of rules.
