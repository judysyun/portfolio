About me!


Judy Yun is a creative at heart.
After graduating from McGill University with a BSc in Computer Science, she realized that working in tech just wasn't for her. She found herself designing in her free time for extracurriculars and personal projects and is now finally pursuing her passions! Judy loves creating work that spans from explorations of playful and silly ideas to works associated with social and political issues.

Fun fact: Judy designed and coded this website all from scratch! (I guess that degree wasn't entirely useless)
